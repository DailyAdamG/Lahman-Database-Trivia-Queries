Q: Which outfielders have won a Silver Slugger Award at age 21 or younger?

Code:

SELECT People.nameFirst & " " & People.nameLast AS fullName, AwardsPlayers.yearID
FROM People 
INNER JOIN AwardsPlayers 
ON People.playerID = AwardsPlayers.playerID
WHERE AwardsPlayers.awardID = "Silver Slugger" AND AwardsPlayers.notes = "OF" AND AwardsPlayers.yearID - People.birthYear <22;

A: Mike Trout

Q: Who made the most starts at third base in MLB history? 

Code:

SELECT People.nameFirst & " " & People.nameLast AS fullName, SUM(Fielding.G) AS Games_at_3B
FROM People
INNER JOIN Fielding
ON People.playerID = Fielding.playerID
WHERE Fielding.POS = "3B"
GROUP BY People.nameFirst, People.nameLast
ORDER BY SUM(Fielding.G) DESC

A: Hall of Famer Brooks Robinson (2,870 starts for the Orioles in his 23-year career)

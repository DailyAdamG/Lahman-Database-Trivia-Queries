Q: Who had the most stolen bases from 2010-19?

Code:

SELECT People.nameFirst & " " & People.nameLast AS fullName, SUM(Batting.SB) AS TotalSB
FROM People
INNER JOIN Batting
ON People.playerID = Batting.playerID
WHERE Batting.yearID >= 2010
GROUP BY People.nameFirst, People.nameLast
ORDER BY SUM(Batting.SB) DESC

A: Dee Gordon (330)
